Concert Curator GPT

End-to-end system that turns your music taste into Spotify playlists of upcoming EDM concerts in your area - combining live event data, artist discovery, and automated playlist generation, with a Custom GPT serving as the front-end interface.

⚠️ Currently invite-only / limited access during development. Please email me if you are interested in being a test user.

  • Transforms live event data into Spotify playlists of upcoming concerts
  • Matches artists to your taste using listening history, metadata, and a Custom GPT filtering layer
  • Automates playlist creation, refresh, and scheduling (daily/weekly updates)

Genetic Algorithm Simulation

Fish evolution sim using a genetic algorithm (adapted from PyWorld), inspired by my experience at Haystagg, where I used genetic algorithms to model top user populations.

  • Simulates a predator-prey environment where fish evolve over generations using a genetic algorithm (based on PyWorld)
  • Each fish has a simple 22-bit genome controlling size, speed, vision, and reaction chance
  • Fitness is determined by survival time, with sharks applying constant pressure each generation
  • Shows behaviors emerging over time (smaller fish, better awareness, smarter reactions) without hardcoding them
  • Built with Python + pygame, with a clean separation between simulation loop and genetic logic
